## 🌐 组织简介 <small>| About CPHOS</small>

**CPHOS** 由十余名**国家集训队**成员于 2020 年年末组建，经过 5 年的发展，现已有成员接近两百名。作为公益性组织，我们始终致力于：<br>
<small>**CPHOS** was founded in late 2020 by more than ten members of the **National Physics Training Team**. After five years of growth, our membership has expanded to nearly two hundred. As a non-profit organization, we are committed to:</small>

* 🏗️ 为物理竞赛选手构筑透明开放的交流平台<br>
  <small>Building a transparent and open platform for physics competition participants</small>
* 🚀 为退役选手的全方位发展提供支持与帮助<br>
  <small>Providing comprehensive support for the continued development of retired competitors</small>
* 🩺 为物理竞赛的健康蓬勃发展贡献力量<br>
  <small>Contributing to the healthy and vibrant advancement of physics competitions</small>

自成立以来，CPHOS 已组织公益性模拟考试 **27 次**，参与联考学校逾百所，单次参与人数稳定在千人规模。📢 **"CPHOS物理竞赛联考"** 微信公众号持续发布备考经验、试题简报、物竞快讯等高质量内容。<br>
<small>Since its founding, CPHOS has organized **27** non-profit mock examinations, with over one hundred schools participating and consistently over one thousand students per sitting. 📢 Our WeChat official account **"CPHOS物理竞赛联考"** continuously publishes high-quality content including exam preparation tips, problem briefings, and competition news.</small>

## 🏛️ 组织架构 <small>| Organizational Structure</small>

理事会为最高决策机构，下设五个职能部门。成员工作涵盖**部门任务**与**阅卷协作**，正式成员可享有参与**学术沙龙**等专属权益。<br>
<small>The Board of Directors is the highest decision-making body, with five functional departments beneath it. Members' responsibilities cover **departmental tasks** and **grading collaboration**; full members are entitled to exclusive benefits such as participation in **academic salons**.</small>
